Healthcare

OOUTH website

Olabisi Onabanjo University Teaching Hospital

OOUTH Sagamu is the foremost tertiary health institution in Ogun State. Open to all members of the public, not referral-only. The hospital runs 24-hour Accident and Emergency services and has specialist departments across the full range of medical disciplines. Walk-in outpatient registration is available at the hospital.
